In this session of the Cardano Developer Office Hours, Robert Hever, Managing Director at Charli3, alongside a core developer, present an inside look at the infrastructure powering decentralized data on Cardano and where it is heading next. They cover Push & Pull Oracles, the Cardano API, and Dendrite, as well as open-source strategy, ecosystem alignment, and the vision for 2026. A live walkthrough demonstrates current capabilities, how it works, and what’s coming next.
