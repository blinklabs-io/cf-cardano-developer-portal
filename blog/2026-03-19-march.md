In this session of the Cardano Developer Office Hours, Giovanni Gargiulo explores CIP-113, the emerging standard for programmable tokens on Cardano, unlocking richer token behaviors and enabling features such as compliance controls, freeze and unfreeze capabilities, and KYC-aware token flows for advanced on-chain applications. He covers the details of the CIP-113 standard, followed by a live demo and a Q&A with developers and community members.
