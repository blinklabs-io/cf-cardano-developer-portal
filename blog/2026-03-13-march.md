In this session of the Cardano Developer Office Hours, Matthias Benkort, (Dr.) Roland Kuhn, Eric Torreborre, and Julien Eluard discuss Amaru, the budget proposal, and the importance of node diversity in the Cardano ecosystem. They cover an overview of Amaru, followed by a discussion on the budget proposal and why node diversity matters for Cardano’s resilience and long-term decentralization.
